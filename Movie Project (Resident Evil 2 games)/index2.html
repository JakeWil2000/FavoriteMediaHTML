Welcome to the home page of Resident Evil 2 information made by a fan for fans.

Resident Evil 2, also known as Biohazard 2, first came out in 1997 with as a single-player, alternate-universe-based storyline, meaning that there are two(2) versions of gameplay for the main playable characters. The pair consists of rookie-cop Leon S. Kennedy and Claire Redfield, who help each other out often in the game while facing their own paths with their own nightmares. They encounter fellow survivors and enemy combatants alike.

Resident Evil 2 remake, released in 2019, follows a similar structure to its older counterpart, while tweaks are made to enhance its gameplay and the story itself is left altered. They encounter survivors and enemies that resemble 1997's world while also meeting new faces and greater foes, bringing forth a challenge to its players.
